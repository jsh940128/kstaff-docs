# Employer Overview

Welcome to K-Staff. As an **Employer**, you represent the demand side of our cross-border recruitment platform. You use K-Staff to submit demand signals for highly qualified foreign talent seeking employment in Korea.

K-Staff is a strictly regulated, operator-governed liaison system. To ensure compliance and fairness, **direct communication with candidates is structurally forbidden**. All interactions, interviews, and placements are securely coordinated through the platform and our designated Korea Partners.

---

## The Conditional Trust Model

Protecting candidate data is our highest priority. Employers operate under a "Conditional Trust" model, meaning your access to candidate information scales with your compliance status.

1. **Restricted Default (Tier-1):** By default, all Employers can browse our candidate pool using anonymized data. You will see non-identifying attributes like target role, experience band, and job category.
2. **Trusted Status (Tier-2):** To view structured resumes and detailed background information (Tier-2), your organization must sign the active Master Service Agreement (MSA). Once a Platform Admin verifies your compliance and upgrades your account to `trusted`, you can unlock Tier-2 profiles.
3. **No PII Access (Tier-3):** Employers are permanently barred from viewing Tier-3 data (passports, health certificates, raw contact details). This is handled exclusively by the Platform and your assigned Korea Partner during the final placement stages.

## Platform Walkthrough

Your interaction with the platform follows a streamlined, compliance-first process. Here is how to navigate the Employer Portal.

### 1. Browsing the Candidate Pool (Tier-1)
Once provisioned by our sales team, you can browse the active candidate pool. By default, you will see anonymized Tier-1 data, which includes the candidate's code, target role family, origin country, experience years, and Korean language proficiency.

![Browse Candidates](/img/employer-1.png)

### 2. Requesting Tier-2 Access
If you find a candidate that matches your criteria and want to view their detailed resume, click the **View Resume** button. If you have not yet been granted access to this specific profile, the system will prompt you to submit a Tier-2 Access Request for Platform approval.

![Request Tier-2 Access](/img/employer-2.png)

### 3. Viewing Tier-2 Resumes (Granted Access)
Once the Platform approves your access, you can view the candidate's structured Tier-2 data, including their education and provenanced work experience. For strict regulatory compliance, notice that this access is highly governed: it includes a specific Grant ID and a strict time-bound expiration date. 

![Granted Tier-2 Access](/img/employer-5.png)

### 4. Submitting a Demand Request
When you are ready to formally signal your hiring intent, navigate to the **Create Request** tab. Here, you will specify the Request Type, the exact Role Needed, your required Headcount, and optionally link the specific Candidate Codes you are interested in pursuing.

![Create Demand Request](/img/employer-2.5.png)

### 5. Tracking Your Requests
Once submitted, you can monitor your active and completed pipelines in the **My Requests** dashboard. This provides a high-level view of where your hiring signals are in the platform's review and execution process.

![My Requests Dashboard](/img/employer-3.png)

### 6. Viewing Request Progress
To see granular details, click **View Progress** on any item in your My Requests list. This opens a read-only timeline showing exactly when the request was submitted, its current status (e.g., `Open`, `Reviewing`, `Routed`), and the specific stages of any candidates associated with this demand request.

![Request Progress Detail](/img/employer-4.png)

---

### Next Steps (Platform & Partner Execution)
Once your request is actively moving, the Platform acts as the ultimate gatekeeper, evaluating your request and routing it to a certified **Korea Partner**. This partner acts as your local liaison. They will reach out to coordinate the interview process and handle the final placement locking on your behalf.
